🌿 Water Me 🌿


Hey there! Looking to keep track of your plant's watering cycles? Well, I've got just the thing for you.


My website is designed to update you every six hours on the condition of your plant's soil based on its soil sensor. You can sign up to receive email notifications with your email, or choose to unsubscribe at the bottom of the page at any time. But this website will always be update to date with the most recent update.



This is for you to see the most recent update from the sensor.



If you sign up for notifications with your email below, you will receive updates for when it needs watering, when it is being overwatered, or if it's perfect. Type your desired email below to receive email updates, and press confirm!





If you or someone else decides to unsubscribe, you can easily unsubscribe here. Please enter the email you wish to unsubscribe into the text box above. Then press the unsubscribe button as confirmation.
