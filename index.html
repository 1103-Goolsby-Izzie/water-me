🌿 Water Me 🌿

Hey, this is just for you, so you can remember to water your plants! I know you forget to water them from time to time, and that although you love plants, you can never keep them alive because you forget to water them all the time. So I made this to last all year long without so much as a battery change, and I created this website to allow you to observe live soil updates!



This button is for live monitoring. Press the button to retrieve a live update!



If you sign up for notifications with your email below, you will receive updates for when it needs watering or when it is being overwatered. Type your desired email below to recieve email updates!
